Thank you for your interest in working for RushTeamApparel - official merchandise supplier for the Washington Interscholastic Activities Association.

RushTeamApparel has two goals in working with college age students:

1. To help students reach their goals.

2. To help run our mobile retail apparel sales booths.

Our events are generally happening during sports or scholastic activities on Fridays and Saturdays in the months of November, January, February, March, and May. Please see the exact dates by going to WIAA.com and clicking on "Tournaments".

Generally speaking, college students work with RushTeamApparel because they can make $200 to $350 per event with bonus opportunities available.

Thank you and we look forward to seeing your application.
